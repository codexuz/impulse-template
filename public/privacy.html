Privacy Policy

Impulse Student App — Impulse LC

Effective date: October 16, 2025

Introduction

Impulse LC built the Impulse Student App as an educational mobile application designed to help students learn interactively through AI chat, videos, games, and assignments. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services:

a. Personal Information

  • Name
  • Profile avatar

b. Usage Data

  • App activity (features used, progress, completion data)

c. Optional Data

  • Voice or text responses if you use AI Chat
  • Uploaded homework or files if you submit exercises

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide and personalize learning experiences
  • Track academic progress and achievements
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests
  • Ensure a safe and secure learning environment

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using industry-standard practices. We use encryption and authentication measures to protect your information. While we strive to protect user data, no system is completely secure — in the rare event of a data incident we will follow applicable laws and notify affected users as required.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services that process information to provide and improve features. Examples include but are not limited to:

  • OpenAI / Deepgram — AI chat, speech or pronunciation analysis

Please review the privacy policies of those services for more details.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for students aged 18 and above. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it promptly. Parents or guardians who wish to request deletion should contact us.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access and update your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Withdraw consent for data collection (some features may become unavailable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at impulsestudylc@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
